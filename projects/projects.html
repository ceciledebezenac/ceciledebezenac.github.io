Designing for landscapes in the Vallee de la Seine: urban planning award 2018

I participated in an interdisciplinary urban design project, along with architects, urban planners, economists and sociologists. We were interested in rethinking the development and the management of the territory Vallee de la Seine, stretching out from the heart of Paris to the coasts of Normandy. This diverse area has received some attention as rail and road infrastructure projects are being designed in line with the idea of an ever extending capital region. We suggested a sustainable strategy that takes into account the uncertainties associated with developing projects and the specificities of the triptych river landscape: rural, periurban and urban. This project won first prize 2018 and is available for download here.
