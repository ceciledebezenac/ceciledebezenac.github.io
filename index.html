About

I am a PhD student at the University of Cambridge and the The Alan Turing Institute, the UK’s national institute for data science and artificial intelligence. I am also a Data Scientist in HSBC’s Big Data Lab.

My supervisors are:

  • Vasco M. Carvalho (Faculty of Economics, University of Cambridge)
  • Chenlei Leng (Dept. of Statistics, University of Warwick)
  • Mingli Chen (Dept. of Economics, University of Warwick).

Before joining the Turing, I obtained a BSc in Economics from the University of Muenster, an MSc in Economics from the University of Bonn and an MSc in Statistics from the London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE). During my studies, I spent one semester at Aix-Marseille University.
